      📋 Break Sheet — How It Works
      Read this before you start.
      ⚡ Two Important Things
      1 · Fully live
      Everyone sees every change instantly. No saving. No refreshing.
      2 · Kyiv time only
      Use the clock in the top right. Not your laptop. Not your phone.
      📅 Auto Assignment
      Rows are filled automatically from the schedule. You cannot manually change who is in a row. Agents are added when their shift starts and removed the moment their shift ends — all break data is wiped at that point. The schedule re-checks every minute.
      ② Book Your Break
      You get 60 minutes total across up to 3 slots.
      🟢 Grid (fastest)
      Click your row to activate it. 1st click = start, 2nd click = end.
      ⌨️ Manual
      Type HH:MM — 2 digits for hour, auto-jumps to minutes.
      ③ Start & Stop
      Booking ≠ being on break. Press ▶ Start when walking away. Press ■ Stop when back.
      ⚠️ Rules
      👥 Max 2 agents on break at once — only when 6+ agents are on shift. Max 30 min overlap.
      🍱 No breaks in the last 30 min of shift.
      🕐 Max 60 min total per agent per shift.
